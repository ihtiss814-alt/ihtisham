SECTION 5 - SIMILAR CARS:
Header: "Similar Cars"

Fetch from Supabase:
SELECT * FROM cars 
WHERE make = [current car make]
AND model = [current car model]
AND id != [current car id]
AND status = 'available'
ORDER BY year DESC
LIMIT 6
