CATEGORY: Buying Process

Q: How can I start buying cars from 
Wazir Trading LLC?
A: Follow these 5 simple steps:
1. Search your desired vehicle on our website
2. Click Inquire Now or contact us on WhatsApp
3. Receive your Proforma Invoice from us
4. Make payment via Telegraphic Transfer 
   to our Japan bank account only
5. We arrange shipment and send you 
   Bill of Lading
Visit our How To Buy page for full details.

Q: Who can buy cars from Wazir Trading?
A: Anyone can buy from us including 
automobile dealers and individual buyers 
worldwide. We recommend checking your 
country's import regulations before 
making a purchase.

Q: How many cars do you have available?
A: We maintain a live inventory of 
thousands of quality Japanese used cars 
on our website. We also have access to 
over 100,000 cars weekly through Japanese 
auto auctions including USS, TAA, JAA, 
and AUCNET auction houses.

Q: Can I cancel my purchase order?
A: Yes, cancellation is possible but 
you may need to cover any incurred costs 
as we may need to resell the vehicle. 
Please contact us as soon as possible 
if you wish to cancel.

Q: Do you help find specific cars 
not listed on your website?
A: Yes. Use our "Can't Find Your Car?" 
form on the homepage or contact us on 
WhatsApp with your requirements. We will 
search Japanese auctions to find your 
exact vehicle within your budget.

CATEGORY: Shipping

Q: How long does delivery take?
A: Typical delivery time is 4 to 8 weeks

confirmed we ship on the earliest 
available vessel from Yokohama port.

Q: What shipping methods do you offer?
A: We offer both RORO (Roll-on Roll-off)

your destination country. RORO is 
generally faster and cheaper for 
single vehicles.

Q: Do you ship to my country?

CATEGORY: Auction

Q: How does the Japanese auction process work?
A: Japan has multiple auction houses 
including USS, TAA, JAA, and AUCNET. 
We attend these auctions on your behalf 
and bid on vehicles matching your 
requirements. Auction cars are graded 
by condition (Grade 3 to Grade 5) 
and include detailed inspection sheets.

Q: What do auction grades mean?
A: Japanese auction grades indicate 
vehicle condition:
Grade 5 / 5A: Excellent condition, 
like new, very low mileage
Grade 4.5: Very good condition, 
minor wear only
Grade 4: Good condition, small scratches 
or minor issues
Grade 3.5: Average condition, 
some visible wear
Grade 3: Fair condition, 
noticeable wear or minor damage

Q: How can I know the right bidding 
price for a vehicle?
A: We have access to auction price 
history for the last 3 months which 
shows what similar vehicles have sold 
for. This helps us advise you on a 
competitive and fair bidding price.

CATEGORY: General

Q: Do you inspect cars before shipping?
A: Yes. All cars in our inventory are 
inspected and we verify the auction 
inspection sheet for every vehicle. 
For additional peace of mind you can 
request an independent third-party 
inspection before shipment.

Q: Can I get Left Hand Drive (LHD) cars?
A: LHD cars are rare in Japan but we 
can source them from our network. 
Please contact us with your specific 
LHD requirements and we will search 
for the best options available.

Q: What is FOB price vs C&F price?
A: FOB (Free On Board): Price of the 
car loaded on the ship in Japan. 
You pay shipping separately.
C&F (Cost and Freight): FOB price plus 
shipping cost to your destination port. 
This is the total price to your port.
CIF (Cost Insurance Freight): C&F 
price plus marine insurance coverage.
