SECTION 4 - HOW TO MAKE PAYMENT:
Title: "Step by Step Payment Guide"

Step 1: Get Your Invoice
After confirming your car, request your 
official Proforma Invoice from us via 
WhatsApp or email. Invoice includes 
full bank details and payment amount.

Step 2: Verify Bank Details
Cross-check bank details on invoice 
with the official details on this page. 
If anything is different contact us 
immediately before sending.

Step 3: Make Transfer
Visit your bank and make a Telegraphic 
Transfer (TT) to our Japan account. 
Make sure to include your Invoice Number 
in the transfer reference.

Step 4: Send Payment Receipt
After transfer, send us a clear photo 
or scan of your bank payment receipt 
via WhatsApp. This confirms payment 
and we begin processing your shipment.
